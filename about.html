Who We Are ?

Our Story

Jenneys Academy “The First Choice for the Future Hotelier”. Choosing where to study can be one of the biggest decisions. In a world of ever increasing change, it is important to pursue an education which not only provides well recognized qualification, but also allows you to gain realistic learning experience and valuable living skills. Jenneys Academy places an emphasis on applied skills based learning and retains close links with the hospitality industry it serves. With the Jenneys Academy qualification one will be in an ideal position to enter the work force and pursue a successful career in the Hospitality Industry anywhere in the world. With highly qualified, experienced and dedicated teachers, the students are trained to fulfil their dreams in the Hospitality sector. We provide holistic learning with importance to the mind, body and personality in order to become integrated personalities.


Our Founder

A Short Story

Thiru L. Adaikalaraj M.P

“To live in the hearts
we leave behind is not to die”

Thiru L. Adaikalaraj, our founder was the member of Parliament, Lok Sabha from Tiruchy constituency for 4 consecutive terms and Vice-President of Tamilnadu congress committee from 2004 till his death in 2012.

It was our founder’s vision to provide education to the masses of society hence he started Jenneys Academy of Hotel Management in the year 1996 under the charitable trust bearing the name of his spouse “Smt. Rani Adaikalaraj Educational and Charitable Trust”. Three other colleges function under the aegis of this charitable trust, namely

  • Jenneys College of Education
  • Jennys College of Nursing
  • Jenneys College of Physical Education

Founder of LA group of companies which has prestigious businesses namely

  • Black Thunder Theme Park
  • Jenneys Residency Hotel, Coimbatore
  • LA Cinemas, Tiruchy

He had been a member in the consultative committees in the ministry of Railways, Civil Aviation, Food processing Industries, Tourism Committee on Petitions and the Silk Board of India.

Blossomed on 9th May, 1936, he left for the heavenly abode at 76 years of age on 27th September 2012. A visionary and a true leader, our founder Thiru L. Adaikalaraj was practical, down to earth, easily accessible and above all, a true and live humanitarian.

A dynamic entrepreneur, our founder had an artistic bend of mind. His special interests were in social service, cultural organisations and industrial development. He donned the role of the president of the Football Association, Badminton Association and many a cultural organisation. The flame that he has lit continues to burn dispelling the darkness of ignorance.
